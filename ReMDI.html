Responsibility Mindset Development Initiative




OUR ORIENTATION PROGRAMMES HERE

This is a School Way's school-based club with the aim of working with students to enable them develop a sense of responsibility towards their education and personal growth and the development of their community while guiding them to develop the 21st century skills.

It is a platform where students are intentionally engaged to develop skills through active participation in community development activities and involvement in enlightening discussions on personal and community development.

Our focus through this initiative is to keep students aware that it is their responsibility, having had an access to school, to ensure they acquire knowledge and develop skills that will enable them to be useful for themselves and their communities in the future. Students develop skills such as problem-awareness, problem-solving, critical thinking, creativity, collaboration, and leadership skills through this initiative.
