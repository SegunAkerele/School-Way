Volunteer


Are you an inspired educator who would love to join us as a field volunteer who helps students in low-income communities understand the essence of their schooling and guide them on how to learn & develop skills to increase their chances of success through education? If yes, thank you for your interest. We love to have you.

Our aim is to see more excellent performance from our schoolchildren in Nigeria by helping them enjoying learning at school, and reshaping the focus of many who are losing hope in schooling and education as the ultimate path to success. This project is enormous, but we can achieve it through your partnership.


About Our Volunteer Programme

Our effort is to be readily available in every part of Nigeria to better serve our schoolchildren without leaving any of them behind. However, this effort will be achieved if you are ready to join us as one of our field partners who we call upon to facilitate of our programmes in schools around their locality having been instructed on how to handle our programmes as learning and learner advisory services providers.

Who Can Join?

Anyone above secondary school age is welcomed to join our list of volunteers.

How Does It Work?

As our field partner, we call on you to facilitate our programmes at schools that request for our services in your locality. We only contact you when such a school is in your locality. We will supply you with the neccessary resources to be used.

Will I Be Paid?

No. It is a volunteering service. However, we shall support you with everything that will make your labour of love and passion enjoyable.

Note: We do offer paid services. You will be paid if you are called to facilitate such a programme.
