Our Goal

We exist to properly spell out to students how school operates
and how they can maximize the benefits school offers for them to achieve
quality education that will enable them to achieve a sustainable future.

We believe in school system as the main viable means to achieve a sustainable education and future, and our aim is to help students to achieve success in life through that means.

School Way is a student-centered organisation that
focuses on helping students navigate school system
with full understanding of their presence and how
to learn gainfully there through which we solve the
problem of low learning, low self-esteem, low future
aspiration, and school withdrawal among students in
low-income communities of Nigeria.

70% of students in Nigeria are not learning - UNICEF. This educational challenge along
the lack of positive future aspiration, low self-esteem, and school withdrawal are all big
problems affecting the educational performance of students in low-income communities
in Nigeria. However, our belief is:

ONE

If we address the root challenges causing these problems among our students,

TWO

They will open their minds to learning in school and also promote self-education at home;

THREE

These will, in turn, lead to quality knowledge acquisition and skills development.
