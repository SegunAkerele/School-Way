What We Do

Every system has its culture and ways that the clients must understand and follow if they must greatly benefit from the system. School system is not different in this regards, and that is why we are out there to help students in low-income communities at all level of formal education understand the culture and ways of school system for them to gainfully learn there.

Schoolchildren need to understand the culture, the ways, the nature, and essence of school in general for them to make sense of everything that is being done there which is for their growth and development.

Here is what H. B. Drake thought abouts school system & students when he wrote in 1939:

The student is like a traveler; but before a man can travel with enjoyment and profit, he needs both preparation and experience. First he must understand the language of the people among whom he is to travel. Second, if he is wise, he must on arriving, find a reliable guide who can point out to him the chief features of distinction and interest. After this he will be equipped to wander by himself and make his discoveriesâ€¦ otherwise he will be bewildered and go astray.

H. B. Drake


Why You Should Partner With Us

How many of schoolchildren in our low-income communities today understand the essence of their schooling? How many of those who know the essence know how to achieve the aim? How many of them are making meaningful discoveries on their own? We consider we all know that many students today are going astray. But we can change the narrative if we collectively act.

Hellen Keller once said, "Alone we can do so little; together we can do so much." Over the years, in our little strength and limited resources, we have reached over 5000 students in different low-income communities in Ondo and Lagos states through our various platforms. That is a low number given the number of students across all the schools in Nigeria.

Our goal is to reach the students in all local areas of Nigeria. However, considering our capacity without your partnership, it would be long to reach the goal.
