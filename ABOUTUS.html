About Us



We are educators, authors, educational games inventors, and educational initiatives initiators who not only inspire and motivate young people to go to school but also provide them with plausible reasons why they must, teach them how to gain there, and as well give thoughtful answers to their controversial questions and logical explanations to their erroneous beliefs that disvalue schooling and education in their minds, and guide them to achieve the very best.





OUR CONCERN


Schooling and education no longer have value to lots of young people. The old strategy of saying "education" is the ultimate way to success in life to motivate them to focus in school is no longer effective because many half-told life stories have proven this truth to be a lie.

Why would school-age young people not want to attend school or focus on their studies?

  • Young people are facing real-life situations and cannot understand how school plays its role in helping to overcome them: most schools think it is unnecessary to address this issue.
  • Young people are confused about the meaning of education and how to attain it most conveniently: they think "going to school" and education means the same.
  • Students in school do not know how to learn there, and that's not part of any school curriculum in Nigeria.
  • Young people do not know how to develop the skills needed to thrive as educated young people in society: school hardly bothers to teach their students "how-to knowledge" regarding skills development.
  • There are so many negative voices against schooling and education by dropouts and unschooled celebrities, which have not been well explained to students and have not been reasonably refuted in their minds.
  • And many more reasons.

WHO WE ARE


School Way is a student-centered organisation that focuses on helping students navigate school system with full understanding of their presence and how to learn gainfully there through which we solve the problem of low learning, low self-esteem, low future aspiration, and school withdrawal among students in low-income communities of Nigeria.

We make learning processes understandable for students, address their controversial thoughts and questions that disvalue schooling and education in their minds, and guide them to develop skills that will enable them succeed after school.


OUR VISION

To see more young people succeed in life through education.

MISSION

To properly spell out to students how school operates and how working education can be attained for them to achieve meaningful success as a result of their education.

OUR BELIEFS

  • We believe in education as the most significant advantage to have.
  • We believe in the school system as the most effective learning and developing platform for education.
  • We believe that schools considered to be average can raise great men and women if the students get the needed help to learn.
  • We believe in self-education as the fastest means to achieve personal growth.
  • We believe that knowledge and skills are the most needed tools to achieve greatness.
  • We believe in students' readiness and awareness as the best way for schools to make an impact.
  • We believe in partnership as the only means to impact society profoundly.
  • As Christians, we believe in Jesus Christ as a true path to God who is the primary source of knowledge and wisdom.
